Data Sources & Information

This map visualizes combined vulnerability in Barcelona based on three primary factors:

1. Combined Heat (UHI + LST): An average index derived from the Urban Heat Island (UHI) effect and Land Surface Temperature (LST) data.

2. Drought (SPEI): Standardized Precipitation Evapotranspiration Index, indicating hydrological stress.

3. Vulnerable Population: Data on specific vulnerable demographic groups (e.g., population aged 65+).

All data points are aggregated to local neighborhood areas and normalized to create a composite vulnerability score (0-1).
